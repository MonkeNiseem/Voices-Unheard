About Voices Unheard

We created Voices Unheard because we want to give a platform for good artists who don't have exposure. This group allows us to educate people around the world on various different types of artists and give people new tastes of music they can try out. We think over time, once our group keeps growing, we can create a strong network of people who want to learn more about artists.
